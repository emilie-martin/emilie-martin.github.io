Space Concordia

January 2019 - June 2022

 Spacecraft Division - Recruitment Officer

March 2021 - June 2022

Organized, hosted and presented info sessions for students interested in Space Concordia; more specifically the Spacecraft Division. During these presentations, I went over a general overview of the student club, the current project under works, and the various subsystems we have. Charged with emailing interested members with their intro tasks and answering any questions they had. Made sure that the completed intro tasks were well received by the appropriate team leads, and forwarded to the internal lead for account creation and the subsequent onboarding steps.

 Spacecraft Division - Internal General Member

May 2020 - June 2022

Helped write, proofread, and edit the Space Concordia's Spacecraft Division official documentation. Helped translate official texts (internal documentation, sponsorship packages, social media captions). Created promotional content (infographics, social media posts, graphic design, captions/text) for the Spacecraft Division.

 Space Concordia - Logistics and Volunteering Co-coordinator

June 2020 - January 2022

Managed volunteers for outreach events and provided them with all the necessary information they needed. Created interest forms and managed their responses for both internal and external volunteering events.

 Space Concordia - Outreach General Member

June 2020 - January 2022

Presented the club and its projects (all divisions) during outreach events meant to increase club visibility. Interacted with children, students, and industry professionals who had questions about our club and our projects.

 Spacecast - Podcast Member

March 2021 - June 2022

Was one of the founding members of Space Concordia's podcast initiative. Was co-host or a real-time observer for podcast episodes, as well as performed preliminary research on episode topics.

 Spacecraft Division - Command and Data Handling General Member

January 2019 - January 2021

Performed initial research and trade studies for components and technology to be used for the project kick-off. Discussed flight software architecture and helped write work packages.
